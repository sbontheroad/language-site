How It Works

1. Search for an instructor

Read instructor profiles and see reviews. You can also narrow down instructors by hourly rate.

2. Schedule your lesson

Your lesson will be over a skype video call at a time that is convenient for you. You can schedule a lesson in the future or see who is available now.

3. Rate your instructor

Then rate your lesson and provide feedback for your instructor. You can schedule another lesson with the same instructor or try someone new.
